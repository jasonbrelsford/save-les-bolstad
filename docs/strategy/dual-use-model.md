# Dual-Use Model: Golf Course + Public Park

## Concept

Les Bolstad can be both a functioning golf course AND a community park/green space. This dramatically broadens the coalition of support beyond golfers to the entire surrounding community.

## Time-Based Zones

### During Golf Season (April - October)
| Time | Golf Areas | Public Areas |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| Dawn - 10am | Full course for golf | Perimeter walking path, clubhouse café |
| 10am - 6pm | Full course for golf | Perimeter path, practice green (when available), clubhouse |
| 6pm - Dusk | Twilight golf (back 9 only) | Front 9 open for walking, sunset events |
| After dusk | Course closed | Stargazing events, path walking (lit sections) |

### Off-Season (November - March)
| Activity | Location |
|----------|----------|
| Cross-country skiing | Designated loops on fairways |
| Snowshoeing | Rough areas and perimeter |
| Winter walking | Cleared paths |
| Ice skating | Flooded practice green (if feasible) |
| Community gardens | Designated plots near maintenance area |
| Snow sculpture events | Open areas |

## Permanent Public Areas (Always Accessible)

1. **Perimeter Walking Loop** (~1.5 miles)
   - Paved path around the course boundary
   - Benches, interpretive signage about course ecology
   - Dog-friendly (leashed)
   - ADA accessible

2. **Clubhouse & Café**
   - Open to public regardless of golf
   - Community meeting space
   - Local food/coffee
   - Patio with course views

3. **Practice Green & Putting Area**
   - Free public use when not scheduled for lessons
   - "Try Golf" beginner zone

4. **Nature Area / Pollinator Garden**
   - Rough areas managed for wildflowers and native plants
   - Educational signage
   - Bird watching spots

## Non-Golf Programming

### Regular Programs
- **Morning Yoga on the Fairway** (6am, before golf starts)
- **Nature Walks** (led by U of M ecology students)
- **Community Garden Plots** (seasonal)
- **Outdoor Fitness Classes** (boot camps, running groups)
- **Photography Walks** (sunrise/sunset)

### Special Events
- **Stargazing Nights** (dark sky advantage over urban areas)
- **Music on the Green** (summer concert series)
- **Farmers Market** (clubhouse parking lot, weekends)
- **Kids' Golf Clinics** (summer camps)
- **Holiday Events** (Easter egg hunt, fall festival, winter lights)

## Precedent: Courses That Work as Parks

| Course | Location | Model |
|--------|----------|-------|
| Jackson Park | Chicago, IL | Municipal course with public park integration |
| Bethpage State Park | Farmingdale, NY | State park with 5 courses + trails + picnicking |
| Torrey Pines | San Diego, CA | City-owned course in a public park setting |
| Harding Park | San Francisco, CA | Public course with running/walking trails alongside |

## Revenue From Public Use

- Café/restaurant sales to non-golfers
- Event space rentals (concerts, weddings, corporate)
- Community garden plot fees ($50-100/season)
- Cross-country ski pass sales (winter)
- Yoga/fitness class fees
- Farmers market vendor fees
