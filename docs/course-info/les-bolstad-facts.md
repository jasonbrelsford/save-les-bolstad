# Les Bolstad Golf Course — Key Facts

## History
- **Founded**: 1929
- **Named After**: Les Bolstad, legendary U of M golf coach (1946-1975)
- **Location**: 2275 Larpenteur Avenue West, Falcon Heights, MN 55113
- **University**: University of Minnesota Twin Cities
- **Holes**: 18-hole regulation course
- **Par**: 71
- **Yardage**: ~6,100 yards
- **Course Type**: Public university course

## Significance
- Home course of the University of Minnesota Golden Gophers men's and women's golf teams
- One of the oldest university golf courses in the Big Ten
- Affordable public golf option in the Twin Cities metro
- Training ground for generations of Minnesota golfers
- Green space and recreational asset for the Falcon Heights/St. Paul community
